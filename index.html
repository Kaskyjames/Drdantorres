About Dr. Danielo Torres

With a global reputation for precision and innovation, Dr. Danielo Torres is a board-certified Plastic and Orthopedic Surgeon known for merging advanced surgical science with artistic finesse.

Having performed numerous successful surgeries across Europe, Africa, and Asia, Dr. Torres is recognized for delivering life-changing results and deeply personalized care. His mission? To restore confidence, function, and elegance — one patient at a time.

Based in Portugal and consulting worldwide, Dr. Torres combines elite surgical training, technological innovation, and heartfelt dedication to every case.
